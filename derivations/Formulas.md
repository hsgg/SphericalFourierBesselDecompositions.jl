Transform from $(n,l,m)\to(r,\hat r)$:

$$
\delta(r,\hat{\mathbf{r}})
=
\sum_{lm}
\, Y_{lm}(\hat{\mathbf{r}})
\sum_n
\, g_{nl}(r)
\, \delta_{nlm}
=
\sum_{lm}
\, Y_{lm}(\hat{\mathbf{r}})
\, \delta_{lm}(r)
$$

Transform from $(r,\hat r)\to(n,l,m)$:

$$
\delta_{nlm}
=
\int d^2\hat r
\, Y^*_{lm}(\hat{\mathbf{r}})
\int dr\,r^2
\, g_{nl}(r)
\, \delta(\mathbf{r})
$$
